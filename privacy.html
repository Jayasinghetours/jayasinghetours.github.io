Privacy Policy

How we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you make an inquiry through our booking form or WhatsApp, we collect basic personal information necessary to arrange your tour. This includes your Name, Contact Number (WhatsApp), Email Address, and Travel Dates.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to process your booking, arrange your pickup, and communicate with you regarding your tour in Sri Lanka. We do not use your information for marketing spam.

3. Data Protection

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information to third parties. All direct communication is handled securely between our management team and you.

Our website may contain links to external sites (like WhatsApp). Please note that these external sites have their own privacy policies, and we do not accept any responsibility or liability for their policies.
