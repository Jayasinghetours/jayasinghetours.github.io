Terms and Conditions of Vehicle Hire

This document constitutes a binding legal agreement between Jayasinghe Tours and car rental ("Company") and the individual or entity signing the rental contract ("Renter"). By taking possession of the vehicle, the Renter agrees to be bound by the following standard terms and conditions.

1. VEHICLE OPERATION: The Renter shall operate the vehicle in a safe and responsible manner, complying strictly with all Sri Lankan traffic laws, statutory regulations, and local ordinances.

2. VEHICLE CONDITION AND CLEANLINESS: The Renter acknowledges receipt of the vehicle in good working order and agrees to return the vehicle in the exact same condition as received, ordinary wear and tear excepted. Smoking and the transportation of pets are strictly prohibited inside the vehicle. A professional cleaning fee of Rs. [Amount] will be assessed for any violations of this clause.

3. ACCIDENT REPORTING: In the event of any accident, collision, or property damage, the Renter must immediately notify the Company and, where legally mandated, the designated Insurance provider and appropriate police authorities. Failure to report may void coverage.

4. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE: The Renter assumes full financial responsibility for any loss or damage to the vehicle during the rental period, up to the maximum amount of the security deposit and/or insurance excess, subject strictly to the limitations and exclusions of the prevailing insurance policy terms.

5. OPERATIONAL COSTS AND FEES: The Renter accepts sole responsibility for all operational expenses incurred during the rental period, including but not limited to fuel costs, parking fees, toll fees, traffic violations, fines, and towing charges. Puncture repairs and resulting tire damage are the exclusive financial responsibility of the Renter.

6. MILEAGE LIMITATIONS AND PROHIBITED USE: The rental agreement includes a daily utilization limit of 120km (unless an unlimited mileage package has been explicitly contracted). Any excess mileage shall be billed at a rate of Rs. 100 per kilometer. The vehicle shall under no circumstances be utilized for illegal purposes, driven within designated Safari areas, operated off-road, or driven by any individual under the influence of alcohol, narcotics, or prescription medication that impairs driving ability.

7. AUTHORIZED OPERATORS: The vehicle may only be legally operated by the primary Renter or an authorized additional driver formally registered with the Company, provided such individual holds a valid Sri Lankan driving license or an officially recognized International Driving Permit valid in Sri Lanka.

8. ROUTINE MAINTENANCE: For rental periods exceeding three (3) consecutive days, the Renter assumes the duty to perform basic preventative maintenance checks, including verification of engine oil, water/coolant levels, and tire pressure. The Company retains responsibility for mechanical failures not originating from Renter negligence or misuse.

9. UNAUTHORIZED MODIFICATIONS: The Renter is expressly prohibited from installing, modifying, altering, or removing any mechanical parts, exterior fixtures, or interior accessories. Any breach of this provision shall require the Renter to finance the full restoration of the vehicle to its factory condition.

10. RETURN POLICY AND LATE FEES: The Renter agrees to surrender the vehicle to the Company's designated location on or before the agreed-upon rental termination date and time. A standard grace period of one (1) hour is permitted; failure to return the vehicle within this timeframe will result in the assessment of a late penalty fee of Rs. [Amount] per hour.

11. SECURITY DEPOSIT: A security deposit is mandatory prior to dispatch. Said deposit shall be refunded upon the conclusion of the rental period, strictly subject to a comprehensive vehicle inspection for damages and the subsequent deduction of any outstanding financial obligations, penalties, or damages.

12. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY FOR PERSONAL PROPERTY: The Company disclaims any and all liability for the loss, theft, or damage of personal property, valuables, or luggage left unattended in the vehicle during the active rental period or following the vehicle's return.
