Rental Agreement

Car Rental without Driver Policies

1. Vehicle Operation:

The Renter shall operate the vehicle in a safe and responsible manner, complying with all Sri Lankan traffic laws and regulations.

2. Vehicle Condition:

    The Renter agrees to return the vehicle in the same condition as received, including cleanliness. Smoking and pets are strictly prohibited inside the vehicle; a professional cleaning fee of Rs. [Amount] will apply for any violations.

3. Driver Accommodation & Meals

Our drivers take care of their own meals. Regarding accommodation, most tourist hotels in Sri Lanka provide free "Driver Quarters". In the rare event a hotel does not provide this, a small overnight fee of $10-$15 per night may be required to cover the driver's nearby lodging. This will be communicated clearly before booking.

4. Driving Hours

To ensure the safety of both you and our drivers, standard driving hours are from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Night driving is generally avoided unless for specific airport transfers or pre-arranged schedules.
