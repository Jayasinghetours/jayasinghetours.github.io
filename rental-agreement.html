Rental Agreement

Car Rental without Driver Policies

1. Vehicle Operation:

The Renter shall operate the vehicle in a safe and responsible manner, complying with all Sri Lankan traffic laws and regulations.

2. Vehicle Condition:

    The Renter agrees to return the vehicle in the same condition as received, including cleanliness. Smoking and pets are strictly prohibited inside the vehicle; a professional cleaning fee of Rs. [Amount] will apply for any violations.

3. Accidents:

In case of accident, the Renter must immediately notify the Company and, where applicable, the Insurance provider and/or police authorities.

4. Liability & Insurance:

The Renter is responsible for any damage to the vehicle during the rental period, up to the amount of the security deposit and/or insurance excess, subject to the insurance policy terms.

5. Operational Costs:

The Renter is responsible for all fuel costs, parking fees, toll fees, traffic violations, and fines. Puncture repairs are the responsibility of the Renter.

6. Mileage & Usage:

The rental includes a daily limit of 120km or unlimited millage. Any excess mileage will be charged at a rate of Rs. 100 per kilometer. The vehicle shall not be used for illegal purposes, driven off-road, or under the influence of alcohol or drugs,Not allowed for Saffari and offroads ect..

7. Authorized Drivers:

The vehicle may only be driven by the Renter or an authorized driver holding a valid Sri Lankan driving license or a recognized Srilanka Driving Permit.

8. Maintenance:

For rentals exceeding 3 days, the Renter is responsible for checking oil, water/coolant levels, and tire pressure. The Company is responsible for mechanical repairs not caused by Renter negligence.

9. Modifications:

The Renter shall not install, modify, or remove any parts or accessories. Any breach requires the Renter to restore the vehicle to its original condition at their own expense.

10. Return Policy:

The Renter agrees to return the vehicle to the designated location on or before the rental end date. A grace period of 1 hour is allowed; returns after this will incur a late fee of Rs. [Amount] per hour.

11. Security Deposit:

The deposit will be refunded upon return of the vehicle, subject to a full inspection for damages and deduction of any outstanding charges.

12. Personal Property:

The Company is not responsible for the loss of or damage to any personal property left in the vehicle during or after the rental period.
